Facing Foreclosure? You Still Have Options.

If you've fallen behind on payments or received a notice of default, time is critical — but you're not out of options. We help homeowners sell before the auction and walk away with dignity and money in hand.

Pre-Foreclosure

Behind on mortgage payments? We can make you a cash offer and close before the bank forecloses. You keep your credit intact and avoid the public auction process.

Notice of Default

Already received a NOD? The clock is ticking, but there's still time. We move fast — often closing within 14 days to beat your deadline.

Underwater Mortgage

Owe more than your home is worth? We specialize in creative deal structures like subject-to financing that can solve your situation even when traditional sales can't.

Behind on Property Taxes

Tax liens adding up? We can purchase your property and handle the back taxes at closing, so you walk away clean with no lingering debt.
